Postgraduate research contacts

The nature of the postgraduate research information you require will determine who you should contact at The University of Manchester. The following contact details will help you find the information you need.

Academic School contacts

If you have a query about a specific research area, or if you've already made an application, please contact the relevant academic School at the University directly. You can find contact details in the menu below.

Faculty offices

Contact Faculty offices for support with finding School contacts, Centres for Doctoral Training, queries relating to policy and procedure and local funding opportunities.

  • Faculty of Biology, Medicine and Health
