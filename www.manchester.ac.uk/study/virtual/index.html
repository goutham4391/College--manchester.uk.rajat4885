Virtual Manchester

Explore interactive content and experience our University wherever you are.

There is so much for you to discover at Manchester and we've created a host of virtual content that allows you to explore the University at a time, place and pace that suits you.

Visit UoM app

The University of Manchester app gives you the chance to take a self-guided tour of campus. In addition to the tour, you'll find student profiles and course information.  

Download the app on the App Store or Google Play.

If you are taking a self-guided tour on campus please adhere to current social distancing guidelines and current government advice.
