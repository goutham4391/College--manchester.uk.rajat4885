Contact us - undergraduate

If you're unable to find the answer to your question about undergraduate admissions, please contact us in one of the following ways:

Contact the admissions team for your course

If you have a query about a specific course or an ongoing application, contact the admissions team within the relevant academic School at the University. You can find School contact details in the menu below.

Contact us with a general study enquiry

For general questions relating to studying at Manchester that do not relate to specific courses or applications, contact our general study enquiries team.
